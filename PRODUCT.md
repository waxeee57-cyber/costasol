# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

**Public site:** Wealthy tourists and high-net-worth visitors on the Costa del Sol. They are mid-holiday, on a phone or tablet, comparing options. They expect concierge-level service, not a booking engine. They respond to quiet confidence, not loud persuasion.

**Admin panel:** The owner/operator, managing the business from a phone while in the field. Speed, clarity, and zero ambiguity matter more than aesthetics. The interface must work reliably on a small screen in sunlight.

## Product Purpose

Luxury car rental, Marbella. Two cars at launch. The model is concierge: customer inquires, admin confirms via WhatsApp, customer pays at pickup. No payment gateway. The site's job is to build enough trust that the customer sends a message — not to complete a transaction.

## Brand Personality

Understated, confident, luxurious. The brand does not shout. It does not use exclamation marks. It speaks in short, assured sentences. The cars are the hero; the brand is the frame.

## Anti-references

- Generic car rental (Hertz, Avis, Europcar): loud sale banners, price matrices, synthetic cheerfulness, "BOOK NOW" in all caps. The opposite of this brand.
- Any interface that commoditises the experience with filters, grids, and comparisons as the primary mode.

## Design Principles

1. **The car is the product** — Interface elements exist to present the cars, not to compete with them. Restraint in layout creates space for the photography to land.
2. **Trust through calm** — Luxury customers are skeptical of effort. Design that tries too hard signals insecurity. Every screen should feel like it had nothing to prove.
3. **Mobile is the real context** — Both the customer browsing on holiday and the admin working in the field are on phones. Nothing designed desktop-first that degrades to mobile.
4. **Directness over decoration** — Clear labels, honest copy, no placeholder text left in. Every word and every element earns its place.
5. **One action per screen** — The customer has one decision to make per view. The admin has one task at a time. Don't offer alternatives to the primary action.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA as the floor. Mobile-first touch targets (48px min). Admin panel must be operable in field conditions: sunlight, one hand, time pressure. Reduced-motion consideration given animation use on the public site.
